# Seed-Stage SaaS GTM Strategy & Series A Readiness

Partnered with a seed-stage SaaS startup to define their Ideal Customer Profile (ICP), redesign their pricing model, and shape their product roadmap. Delivered a full go-to-market (GTM) playbook and investor-ready technical architecture documentation that supported their Series A raise.

## Results

| Metric | Outcome |
|---|---|
| Series A | Closed within 4 months |
| ICP | Defined & validated |
| Pricing model | +40% ARR increase |

## Engagement Overview

The startup had early product-market signal but lacked a validated ICP, a defensible pricing model, and the documentation investors expect during technical diligence. This engagement combined customer research, pricing strategy, and technical writing to produce the assets needed to scale go-to-market and close a Series A round.

## ICP Definition & Validation

- **Customer interviews**: Structured discovery calls across existing customers and target prospects, segmented by firmographic data (company size, industry, tech stack).
- **Cohort analysis**: Analyzed usage data and retention by segment to identify which customer profiles had the highest activation, retention, and expansion rates.
- **ICP scoring framework**: Built a weighted scoring model (firmographic + behavioral signals) to qualify inbound leads and prioritize outbound targeting.
- **Validation loop**: Tested the ICP hypothesis against win/loss data from the existing sales pipeline to confirm fit before rolling into GTM planning.

## Pricing Model Redesign

- **Willingness-to-pay research**: Conducted Van Westendorp-style pricing surveys and competitive benchmarking across the ICP segment.
- **Packaging restructure**: Moved from a single flat-rate plan to tiered packaging (usage-based + seat-based hybrid) aligned to ICP segment value drivers.
- **Value metric selection**: Identified and implemented a value metric that scaled with customer outcomes rather than just usage volume.
- **Result**: New pricing and packaging structure drove a **40% increase in ARR** without a proportional increase in CAC.

## Product Roadmap

- Reprioritized the roadmap around features that mapped directly to ICP pain points identified during discovery.
- Sequenced roadmap items to support the new pricing tiers — ensuring higher-tier features justified the pricing delta.
- Built a roadmap narrative suitable for both internal alignment and investor presentation.

## GTM Playbook

- **Positioning & messaging**: Defined core value proposition and messaging framework tailored to the validated ICP.
- **Sales motion**: Documented qualification criteria, sales process stages, and objection-handling guides aligned to the new pricing tiers.
- **Channel strategy**: Identified and prioritized acquisition channels based on ICP research (outbound, content, partnerships).
- **Metrics framework**: Defined the KPIs (CAC, LTV, payback period, NRR) the team would track post-raise.

## Investor-Ready Technical Architecture Documentation

- **System architecture diagrams**: Documented the platform's architecture, data flow, and infrastructure for technical due diligence.
- **Scalability narrative**: Articulated how the architecture supports growth assumptions in the financial model.
- **Security & compliance overview**: Summarized data handling, access controls, and compliance posture relevant to enterprise buyers and investors.
- **Roadmap-to-architecture mapping**: Connected the product roadmap to underlying technical work, giving investors a clear view of execution risk.

## Outcome

- ICP defined, validated against pipeline data, and operationalized into lead scoring
- Pricing model redesign delivered a 40% ARR increase
- Combined GTM playbook and technical documentation package directly supported investor diligence
- Series A round closed within 4 months of engagement kickoff
